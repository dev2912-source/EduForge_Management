# Admin Dashboard Educational Theme Design

## 1. Campus Hero Banner (Header)
- Transform the standard "Dashboard / Welcome back" header into an **Academic Hero Banner**.
- **Visuals:** A full-width `bg-white/90` glass banner with a subtle, abstract SVG background pattern (like a library or campus architecture watermark).
- **Typography:** The "Welcome back" text will use a refined, slightly larger font to feel like a university portal.

## 2. Academic Iconography & Patterns (Stats Grid)
- **Icons:** 
  - Students: Swap `Users` for `GraduationCap` (Lucide icon).
  - Staff: Swap `UserSquare2` for `BookOpen` or `Library`.
  - Finance: Swap `IndianRupee` and `AlertCircle` for `Landmark` (representing the institution) and `FileWarning`.
- **Backgrounds:** Inject a faint "graph paper" or "dot grid" CSS pattern (opacity ~2%) into the white glassmorphic cards to mimic a student's notebook or ledger.

## 3. Textbook Motif (Card Accents)
- **Bookmarks:** Replace the current curved color blobs in the top-right of the stats cards with CSS-drawn "bookmark ribbons" that drape over the top edge of the card.
- **Typography:** The primary statistic numbers (e.g., student count, revenue) will be styled with a classic academic feel to resemble textbook chapter headers, while keeping the rest of the text clean and sans-serif for readability.

---
*This design retains 100% of the current data structure and widgets, focusing entirely on visual CSS/iconography changes.*
